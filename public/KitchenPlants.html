KITCHEN INDOOR PLANTS

Snake Plant

The snake plant (Mother-in-law's tongue) is a hardy succulent that thrives in low light and needs minimal watering. Perfect for modern dining spaces.

Aloe Vera

Aloe vera is a low-maintenance, air-purifying plant that also provides healing gel. It fits beautifully in warm, sunlit corners of dining rooms.

Peace Lily

Peace lilies bring elegance with glossy leaves and white blooms. They clean the air and add a calm, graceful vibe to your dining area.
